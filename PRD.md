# Weather Widget — PRD

**What is this?** A weather widget that shows the current temperature, time, and location with a moody, minimal aesthetic.

**Who is it for?** Anyone who wants a beautiful, glanceable weather page.

**What does it do?**
- Shows current temperature for a chosen city
- Displays current time
- Shows city and country name
- Dark, atmospheric card design with rounded corners
- Uses an illustrated Nordic landscape as the card background

**What does it NOT do?**
- No forecasts, no hourly breakdown (for now)
- No weather icons or condition text
- No settings or city search
